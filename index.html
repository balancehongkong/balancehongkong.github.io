ABOUT US

Balance is a Hong Kong student organization with the mission to encourage gender equality and female empowerment by connecting pioneers of the past, present, and future and by supporting young trailblazers to lead our generation towards an equal future. Our upcoming " Balance: Networking Night " aims to serve as a symbolic encouragement to individuals, both male and female alike, to become ambassadors of social change. We hope that participants will be inspired to further raise awareness about gender inequality, break gender stereotypes, and address the gender imbalance in career fields that encounter gender discrepancies.
